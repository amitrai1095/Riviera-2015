CONTEST GUIDELINES

  • The design must incorporate the Riviera 2015 logo. [click here to download]
  • All designs should be at least 300dpi.
  • The design must be your own original, unpublished work and must not include any third-party logos or copyrighted material. By entering the competition, you agree that your submission is your own work.

    • SUBMITTING A DESIGN

  • Only digital entries will be accepted.
  • Designs must be saved as a High Resolution JPG, EPS OR PDF.
  • Email your design to rivieravituniversity@gmail.com
  • Only designs submitted before 20/10/14 will be considered.
  • Applicants understand that they are not entitled to any compensation for their submitted work.
  • List your name, contact no, high school in your submission.
    • Note: We recommend using a program such as Adobe Photoshop or Illustrator to create your design. Make sure to create your art at a minimum resolution of 300 dpi and to the approximate size that the design should be printed.
